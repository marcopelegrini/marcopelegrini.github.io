If you know me you know I don't like to display brands. Are they paying me anything to advertise for them? No.

My dealearship gave me nice license plate frames, but they had their dealearship name on them, so I've decided to remove them ans stop being a free billboard.

I've cut the frame top to match the top, filed, sanded, painted, and voilá... looks better now.

Built with:

- Universal Laser Systems Laser Cutter
